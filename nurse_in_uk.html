BECOMING A NURSE IN THE UK

1. Demonstrate English Language Proficiency
IELTS with minimum score of 7 in speaking, listening and reading and 6.5 in writing or OET with minimum grade of B in speaking, listening and reading and C+ in writing
2. Complete NMC Eligibility & Qualification Application
Applicant will create online account and complete eligibility and qualification application. NMC will confirm eligibility for registration within 14 days
3. Take the Computer Based Test (CBT)
CBT assures the NMC that applicant has the right knowledge and skills to practice nursing in the UK Results are available 48 hours after taking the test
4. Attend Direct or Online Interview & Secure Job Offer
Attend Direct or Online Interview & Secure Job Offer Direct/Online interview arranged by Global Nurse Force. Secure job offer and complete pre-employment checks
5. Secure Certificate of Sponsorship from Employer
Employers assign the CoS once the CBT exam is cleared The sponsorship reference number on the CoS is used for the Tier 2 visa application
6. Submit Tier 2 Health & Care Worker Visa Application
Applicant will submit their Tier 2 visa application and provide biometric application Visa is generally approved within 3 weeks
7. Travel to UK
Global Nurse Force will arrange one way flight to the UK, airport pickup, temporary accommodation and orientation. Collect your Biometric Resident Permit. (BRP)
8. Clear the Objective Structured Clinical Exam (OSCE)
OSCE will assess applicant‚Äôs clinical knowledge. Results emailed within 5 working days from test date.
9. Complete NMC Registration
Submit health, character and language evidence. NMC will assess the application within 35 days. Receive Registration Number from NMC (PIN).
10. Work as a Registered Nurse in the UK
