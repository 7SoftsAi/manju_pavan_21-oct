BECOMING A NURSE IN THE USA

1. Meet English Language Proficiency Requirements
Pass IELTS with a minimum overall score of 6.5 or TOEFL (iBT) with a minimum score of 83 for nursing license eligibility.
2. Pass the NCLEX-RN Exam
Register and pass the NCLEX-RN exam, which is required for nurse licensure in the United States.
3. Apply for VisaScreen Certification
Obtain VisaScreen Certification through CGFNS (Commission on Graduates of Foreign Nursing Schools), which verifies your credentials for a US visa.
4. Attend Interviews & Secure Job Offer
Global Nurse Force arranges interviews with US healthcare employers. Secure a job offer and complete pre-employment checks.
5. Obtain Employer Sponsorship
Your employer will sponsor you for an EB-3 (Employment-Based Green Card) or H-1B visa based on your qualifications and job offer.
6. Submit Your Visa Application
Submit your EB-3 or H-1B visa application and complete required documentation for visa issuance. The process can take several months depending on visa type.
7. Travel to the USA
Global Nurse Force will assist with your relocation, including arranging flights, airport pickup, temporary accommodation, and orientation.
8. Begin Your Nursing Career in the USA
Start your professional career as a Registered Nurse in the USA, enjoying opportunities for growth and success.
